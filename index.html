About BTS

A Grammy-nominated South Korean group, BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan, or "Beyond the Scene"), has been enchanting millions of fans worldwide since its debut in June 2013. There are seven members in total: RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin and V. Known as 21st century pop icons, BTS has become a household name thanks to the acclaim they've received for their original and self-produced music, as well as their impressive live performances and engaging fan interactions.
